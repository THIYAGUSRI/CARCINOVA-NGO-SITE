The People Behind CarciNova

CarciNova is led by a passionate group of health professionals, social workers, and volunteers who share one belief — that cancer awareness saves lives.

Our leadership includes

Board of Directors

guiding long-term strategy and ethical operations.

Executive Team

managing daily awareness activities, teleconsultations, and outreach.

Volunteers

the heart of our movement, helping reach each village with compassion.

We collaborate with

Local District Administrator and Health Departments for campaign permissions.

Oncologists & Specialists who offer free or low-cost teleconsultation sessions.

CSR partners and donor institutions supporting awareness and rural outreach.

“Together, We Carry Hope.”
