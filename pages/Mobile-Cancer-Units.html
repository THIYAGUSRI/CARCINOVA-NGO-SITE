Bringing Cancer Awareness on Wheels

Our upcoming project — CarciNova Mobile Cancer Unit — THE FIRST-EVER AI-BASED CANCER SCREENING INITIATIVE — is a promise to reach every corner of rural Andhra Pradesh with awareness, screening, and hope.

Purpose

The mobile unit will travel across villages to:

Display educational videos and animations about early cancer detection Offer basic screening and counselling support Distribute awareness pamphlets and contact information for teleconsultation

Phase 1 Rollout Plan

We plan to begin in the northern Andhra districts, covering:

East Godavari Visakhapatnam Vizianagaram Srikakulam

Rural Engagement Approach

Our outreach model includes:

Audio-visual awareness presentations Small group counselling by trained volunteers Teleconsultation follow-ups post-visit Tracking patient feedback and needs

“If people can’t reach the cancer care, we’ll bring the care to them.”

Future Vision

With sufficient funding and support, CarciNova plans to:

Add more mobile units across the state Build local awareness hubs in major towns Train community volunteers as “Cancer Ambassadors” in every mandal

“Healthcare that moves with the people it serves.”
