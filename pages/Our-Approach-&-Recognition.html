Our Approach

CarsiNova works through a three-step grassroots model:

Awareness:

Conducting village meetings, women’s group discussions, and video sessions to help people understand early symptoms and prevention.

Guidance & Counselling:

Once a potential patient is identified, we guide them through every step — emotionally, mentally, and medically — using teleconsultation and ongoing motivation.

Sustained Support:

We stay connected with families after diagnosis, ensuring continued counselling, education, and positivity.

We don’t refer patients away; we walk beside them.

Recognition & Future Goals

Our dedication has led to growing recognition among local communities and district-level health officers

With increasing support and funding, we aim to:

Deploy mobile cancer units to Northern Andhra rural zones.

Cover at least 100 villages in the first operational phase.

Expand to educational institutions and women’s self-help groups for awareness programs.
